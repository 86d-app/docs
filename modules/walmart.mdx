---
title: "Walmart"
description: "Walmart Marketplace integration for 86d. List products on Walmart and sync orders back for unified fulfillment."
---

The Walmart module connects to Walmart Marketplace via the Marketplace Items and Orders APIs. Listings push from your catalog; sold orders ingest into your 86d order queue.

**Source:** [`modules/walmart`](https://github.com/86d-app/86d/tree/main/modules/walmart) · **npm:** [`@86d-app/walmart`](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@86d-app/walmart)

## Installation

```sh
86d module add walmart
```
