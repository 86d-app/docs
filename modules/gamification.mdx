---
title: "Gamification"
description: "Points, badges, and challenges for an 86d storefront. Reward customer behaviors beyond purchasing."
---

The gamification module rewards customer behaviors with points and badges. Configure rules (sign-up, first purchase, three reviews in a month) and award challenges. Awarded points can roll into `@86d-app/loyalty` so they spend like loyalty points.

**Source:** [`modules/gamification`](https://github.com/86d-app/86d/tree/main/modules/gamification) · **npm:** [`@86d-app/gamification`](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@86d-app/gamification)

## Installation

```sh
86d module add gamification
```

## Components

- `<BadgeShelf customerId={id} />`, `<ChallengeProgress challengeId={id} />`.
