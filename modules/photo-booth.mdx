---
title: "Photo booth"
description: "In-store photo capture for 86d kiosks and events. Customers take photos, get a shareable link, and you collect their email."
---

The photo-booth module turns a kiosk into a branded photo booth. Customers take a photo, optionally apply a frame, and receive a download link via email. Their email and consent are recorded into the newsletter or marketing list of your choice.

**Source:** [`modules/photo-booth`](https://github.com/86d-app/86d/tree/main/modules/photo-booth) · **npm:** [`@86d-app/photo-booth`](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@86d-app/photo-booth)

## Installation

```sh
86d module add photo-booth
```
