---
title: "TikTok Shop"
description: "Connect your TikTok Shop account to sync your 86d catalog and manage in-app purchases from the admin."
---

The TikTok Shop module connects your TikTok Shop account to your 86d catalog via the TikTok Shop API. It pushes products, syncs inventory, and ingests orders into your 86d order queue.

**Source:** [`modules/tiktok-shop`](https://github.com/86d-app/86d/tree/main/modules/tiktok-shop) · **npm:** [`@86d-app/tiktok-shop`](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@86d-app/tiktok-shop)

## Installation

```sh
86d module add tiktok-shop
```
