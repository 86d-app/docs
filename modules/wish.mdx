---
title: "Wish"
description: "List products on the Wish marketplace and sync orders back to 86d for processing."
---

The Wish module syncs your catalog to Wish via the Wish Merchant API. Sold orders ingest into the `@86d-app/orders` queue.

**Source:** [`modules/wish`](https://github.com/86d-app/86d/tree/main/modules/wish) · **npm:** [`@86d-app/wish`](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@86d-app/wish)

## Installation

```sh
86d module add wish
```
