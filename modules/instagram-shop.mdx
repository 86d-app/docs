---
title: "Instagram Shop"
description: "Sync your 86d catalog to Instagram Shopping. Tag products in posts, stories, and reels."
---

The Instagram Shop module syncs your catalog to your Instagram Shop via the Meta Commerce API. Once products are approved by Meta, you can tag them in posts, stories, and reels.

**Source:** [`modules/instagram-shop`](https://github.com/86d-app/86d/tree/main/modules/instagram-shop) · **npm:** [`@86d-app/instagram-shop`](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@86d-app/instagram-shop)

## Installation

```sh
86d module add instagram-shop
```
