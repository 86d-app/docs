---
title: "Gift wrapping"
description: "Add gift-wrap and gift-message options at checkout in 86d. Per-line-item or per-order, with configurable upcharge."
---

The gift-wrapping module adds a gift-wrap upsell at checkout: per-item or per-order wrap, optional gift message, and a configurable upcharge. The packing slip generated for fulfillment includes the message and a flag for the warehouse.

**Source:** [`modules/gift-wrapping`](https://github.com/86d-app/86d/tree/main/modules/gift-wrapping) · **npm:** [`@86d-app/gift-wrapping`](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@86d-app/gift-wrapping)

## Installation

```sh
86d module add gift-wrapping
```
