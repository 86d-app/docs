---
title: "X Shop"
description: "Connect X Shopping to surface 86d products in posts and on your storefront profile."
---

The X Shop module connects your store to X (formerly Twitter) Shopping. It syncs your catalog to X's commerce surface and exposes a checkout link from product posts.

**Source:** [`modules/x-shop`](https://github.com/86d-app/86d/tree/main/modules/x-shop) · **npm:** [`@86d-app/x-shop`](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@86d-app/x-shop)

## Installation

```sh
86d module add x-shop
```
