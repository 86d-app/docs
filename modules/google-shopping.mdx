---
title: "Google Shopping"
description: "Push your 86d catalog to Google Merchant Center for Shopping ads and free listings. Conversion tracking via GTM."
---

The Google Shopping module generates a product feed in Google's expected format and (when configured) pushes it to Google Merchant Center via the Content API. Conversion tracking integrates with `NEXT_PUBLIC_GOOGLE_TAG_MANAGER_ID` for ROAS reporting.

**Source:** [`modules/google-shopping`](https://github.com/86d-app/86d/tree/main/modules/google-shopping) · **npm:** [`@86d-app/google-shopping`](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@86d-app/google-shopping)

## Installation

```sh
86d module add google-shopping
```

## Store endpoints

| Method | Path | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `GET` | `/feeds/google-shopping.xml` | Product feed for Merchant Center |
