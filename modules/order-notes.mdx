---
title: "Order notes"
description: "Threaded notes on orders from customers and admins in 86d. Internal-only or visible to the customer."
---

The order-notes module adds a threaded note feed to every order. Notes can be marked internal (admin-only) or external (visible to the customer in their order history). Useful for special instructions, fulfillment exceptions, and customer support follow-ups.

**Source:** [`modules/order-notes`](https://github.com/86d-app/86d/tree/main/modules/order-notes) · **npm:** [`@86d-app/order-notes`](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@86d-app/order-notes)

## Installation

```sh
86d module add order-notes
```
