---
title: "Social proof"
description: "Real-time purchase counts, viewer counts, and trust badges for 86d storefronts."
---

The social-proof module surfaces signals that build buyer confidence: live purchase notifications, recent-order counts, current-viewer counts on product pages, and configurable trust badges.

**Source:** [`modules/social-proof`](https://github.com/86d-app/86d/tree/main/modules/social-proof) · **npm:** [`@86d-app/social-proof`](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@86d-app/social-proof)

## Installation

```sh
86d module add social-proof
```

## Components

- `<RecentPurchases />`, `<ViewerCount productId={id} />`, `<TrustBadges />`.
