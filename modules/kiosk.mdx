---
title: "Kiosk"
description: "In-store kiosk mode for 86d. Locked-down browsing and checkout for self-service stations."
---

The kiosk module reconfigures the storefront for self-service in-store kiosks: full-screen layout, idle reset, no admin links, optional cash drawer integration through `@86d-app/toast`, and quick-pay flows. Designed for tablets and dedicated hardware in physical retail spaces.

**Source:** [`modules/kiosk`](https://github.com/86d-app/86d/tree/main/modules/kiosk) · **npm:** [`@86d-app/kiosk`](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@86d-app/kiosk)

## Installation

```sh
86d module add kiosk
```
