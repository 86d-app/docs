---
title: "Appointments"
description: "Bookable appointments for 86d. Sell consultations, fittings, classes, and other time-bounded services with calendar availability."
---

The appointments module sells time-bounded services (consultations, fittings, classes, repair drop-offs). Each service has a duration, a price, a per-staff or per-location calendar, and configurable buffers. Customers book at checkout and receive calendar invites by email.

**Source:** [`modules/appointments`](https://github.com/86d-app/86d/tree/main/modules/appointments) · **npm:** [`@86d-app/appointments`](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@86d-app/appointments)

## Installation

```sh
86d module add appointments
```
