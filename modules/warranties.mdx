---
title: "Warranties"
description: "Product warranty plans and claim processing for 86d. Sell extended warranties at checkout and manage claims from the admin."
---

The warranties module sells warranty plans alongside products at checkout (extended coverage, accidental damage, theft) and processes claims through a status flow: `submitted -> reviewing -> approved -> resolved` (with `rejected` as terminal alternate).

**Source:** [`modules/warranties`](https://github.com/86d-app/86d/tree/main/modules/warranties) · **npm:** [`@86d-app/warranties`](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@86d-app/warranties)

## Installation

```sh
86d module add warranties
```
