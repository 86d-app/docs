---
title: "Pinterest Shop"
description: "Sync your 86d catalog to Pinterest as Product Pins so shoppers can discover and buy directly."
---

The Pinterest Shop module syncs your catalog as Pinterest Product Pins. Pinterest pulls the feed on a regular cadence; the module generates and serves it from `/feeds/pinterest.xml`.

**Source:** [`modules/pinterest-shop`](https://github.com/86d-app/86d/tree/main/modules/pinterest-shop) · **npm:** [`@86d-app/pinterest-shop`](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@86d-app/pinterest-shop)

## Installation

```sh
86d module add pinterest-shop
```
